MoThUS ?

MoThUS is a game based on Motus or the famous Wordle. This game will put your brain to the test while improving your knowledge of chemistry, because here, instead of using the letters on the keyboard, we'll be playing with the periodic table of chemical elements.

Rules

You have 8 tries to guess the word of the day, common to all. You can only suggest words that begin with the same letter as the word you are looking for, and that can be found in our dictionary. Proper nouns are not accepted. The word changes every day. So avoid spoilers and use the share button instead.

Letters surrounded by a red square are correctly placed, the letters surrounded by a yellow circle are wrongly placed (but present in the word). Letters with a blue background are not in the word.
